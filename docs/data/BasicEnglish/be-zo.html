'zany, n. [Hist.] Sort of CLOWN; one who
is foolish or feeble-minded. ('zeini).

zeal, n. Warm interest, giving of one's
best work, for cause etc. '-ot ('zelət),
n. One with overmuch zeal for cause
etc. '-ous ('zeiəs), a. (zi:l).

'zebra, n. Horse-like animal of Africa.
banded with light and dark marks on
skin. ('zi:brə).

'zebu, n. Cow-like animal in E. India.
('zi:bu:).

ze'nana, n. (In India) part of house in
which women are kept. (zi'na:nə).

'zenith, n. Point of sky straight over one's
head; highest point of development,
power etc. ('zeniθ).

'zephyr, n. The west wind, soft wind;
sorts of thin dress-material. ('zefə).

'Zeppelin, n. G., sp. military, airship.
(zepəlin).

'zero, n. Number 0, nothing; starting-point
on scale of heat-measuring instrument,
etc. Z. hour, [Mil.] time fixed for
attack. ('ziərou).

zest, n. Great interest or pleasure;
pleasing, biting, taste or quality.
(zest).

'zeugma, [Lang.] n. Placing of 2 words
in the same relation to another which
only goes rightly with one of them (with
smiling lips and hearts). ('zju:gmə).

'zigzag, 1. n. Straight lines joined and
forming a number of equal or unequal
angles, first one way and then the other;
anything in this form. 2. a. Forming a z.
3. adv. In z. direction. 4. v.i. Go z.
('zigzag).

zinc, n. White metal used for roofing, for
coating iron, etc. (ziŋk).

'zinnia, n. Garden-plant with stiff, bright-coloured
flowers. ('ziniə).

'Zionist, n. Supporter of design to make
Palestine a country for the Jews.
('zaiənist).

'zither(n), n. Music instrument with cords
and flat sounding-board played resting

'zodiac, n. Band of the sky outside which
sun, moon, and great moving stars do
not go, marked out into 12 equal
divisions having names of 12 groups of
stars. The signs of the z., the 12 divisions
of the z. used sp. in reading person's
future from the stars. ('zoudiak).

zone, n. Band of colour etc. round something;
any one of the 5 divisions, based
on degree of heat, into which the earth
is marked off (torrid z., frigid zz., temperate
zz.); any part of country, earth,
a space, marked off by certain conditions
or for some purpose (war z., etc.). (zoun).

zoo, [Com.] n. ZOOLOGICAL gardens.
(zu:).

science of their forms, grouping, distribution
etc. zoo'logical (zuə'lɔdʒikl). a.
Sp., z. gardens, place where number of
different sorts of living animals are kept
and put on view. (zou'ɔlədʒi).

'zoophyte, n. Sorts of plant-like sea-animal.
('zouəfait).

zou'ave, n. Man of F.-Algerian REGIMENT.

zounds, [Old] int. Cry of angry protest.

'Zulu, n., a. (Person, language) of certain
black group in S. Africa. ('zu:lu:).
