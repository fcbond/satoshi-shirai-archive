# Satoshi Shirai's Research Website — Archive

This is a preserved copy of the personal research website of **Satoshi Shirai** (白井諭),
a research engineer in natural language processing, who passed away on January 19, 2012.

Original URL: http://www.kinet-tv.ne.jp/~sat/

Archived to preserve his publications and research contributions.

He was my valued colleaugue and friend, and I want to make sure his legacy lives on.

```
wget --mirror \
      --convert-links \
      --adjust-extension \
      --page-requisites \
      --no-parent \

      -P shirai-sat-archive \
      http://www.kinet-tv.ne.jp/~sat/
```
I then deleted the photos of flowers, and the links to them,  moved the papers to `docs/` and removed the traffic analyzer.

I hope this means the papers will be visible forever!
